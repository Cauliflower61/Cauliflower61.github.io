Video Erasure Model Optimization for AIGC Cloud Editing

Optimization of an online video erasure model based on Wan2.2-5B, including paired-data construction redesign and DMD distillation from 30-step inference to 4-step inference.

Video Erasure Wan2.2-5B Paired Data CV Mask Generation DMD Distillation 30-step → 4-step

Overview

Project Summary

Background

The video erasure model was an existing online model fine-tuned from Wan2.2-5B for AIGC cloud editing scenarios.

My Work

Responsible for paired-data construction optimization and DMD-based few-step distillation to improve training data logic and inference efficiency.

Data

Paired Data Construction

Previous Logic

The previous pipeline relied on two videos: one video was used to construct the mask, while another video was used as the ground truth. This made the paired-data logic less natural for the erasure task.

Redesigned Logic

The new pipeline uses CV methods to generate erasure masks and constructs input video, mask, and ground-truth pairs from a single video, improving data controllability and construction stability.

Distillation

DMD Distillation

30-step to 4-step Inference

Applied DMD distillation to compress the original 30-step video erasure model into a 4-step inference model. The distilled model significantly reduces sampling steps while maintaining erasure quality close to the original model.
